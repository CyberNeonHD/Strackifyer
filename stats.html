Welcome to Strackifyer

Beta V0.1

Strackifyer has only recently been released as a beta 0.1 version. Because I'm not sure if things will work smoothly, the first couple of weeks will remain in beta. This version depicts "Stage 1" in its early stages. You can view your top ten artists and top ten songs from the previous two weeks. You'll also be able to see the top three albums in your collection, although keep in mind that this is based on your most recent two weeks of listening. Because this is a beta version, things may still be unstable and errors may occur. This project is still being worked on. From now on, you may expect regular updates on this project.
