What is strackifyer?

Strackifyer is a website that displays your personal Spotify listening statistics without storing any user information. This is a current work-in-progress project, for additional information, see the road map. The features listed below are those that will be available on the site in the future. This is my first significant project, and I plan to do all in my ability to make the design and features as good as possible. Suggestions are always welcome.

Top 10 artists

Do you want to know which artist you're listening to the most? Soon, you'll be able to see your top 10 Spotify artists from the last four weeks, six months, and all-time. This is a first-stage feature. This will be extended to your top 50 in stage 3!

Top 10 tracks

Do you want to know what songs you listen to the most? Soon, you'll be able to view your top 10 Spotify tracks from the previous four weeks, six months, and all-time. This is a first-stage feature. This will be extended to your top 50 in stage 3!

Top 3 albums

Have you ever wondered what your favourite albums are? You'll find out soon enough. Since Spotify does not provide this information, I must calculate it myself. Hence, why it will launch as a beta. This will also extend to your top 50 in stage 3!

Road Map

Stage 1 - In progress 

The ability to log into Spotify and see your own basic statistics. Stats like you top 10 artist and tracks of the past 4 weeks, 6 months and overall stats.
This stage is also seen as beta.

Stage 2

Primarily focused on thinking about more features, improving the design for showcasing the stats, and cleaning some code and optimizing some code, as well as some more back-end code for future implements.

Stage 3

When stage 3 drops, a lot more statistics will come as well! Stats like top 100 artists/tracks and more detailed stats about the artists as well as the tracks themselves.

Stage 4

This is the big stage. I will drop a bunch of extra features. See and edit all of your playlists. Searching for tracks and albums to add to your playlist. Check your recent track history to see what you've been playing. Look up a specific album, artist, or track to see what statistics are available for it and many more features.
