Features

The features below are the future/current features that the site will provide. Spotifyer is a work in progress and is getting worked on. This is my first major project and I will do my best to make the desing and features as good as possible, suggestions are always welcome.

Top 10 artist

Want to see who you listin to the most? Soon you will be able to check your top 10 spotify artist of last 4 weeks, last 6 months and last year. This is a stage 1 feature, in stage 3 this will go to top 50!

Top 10 Tracks

Want to see what song you listin to the most? Soon you will be able to check your top 10 spotify tracks of last 4 weeks, last 6 months and last year. This is a stage 1 feature, in stage 3 this will go to top 50!

Top 3 albums (beta)

Yeah, top 3 doesn't seem much but since spotify doesn't provide this data, I need to calculate it myself. In stage 1 this will be top 3 duo to it being a beta feature. In stage 3 this will go to top 50 as well!

Future features

In the later stages you will be able to check and edit your playlist, check your recent history, check your upcomming "hot" tracks and artist. Search a specific track/album/artist and see the specific stats for it and much more

Current Stages

Stage 1

The start - basic

Log-in to your spotify and check your top 10 artists,tracks of the last 4 weeks, 6 months and last 12 months. You will also be able to see your top 3 albums (beta feature) of the last 4 weeks, 6 months and last 12 months. Spotify doesn't provide the data of your top albums, I need to calculate it myself (that's why I add it in as beta, since there could be errors in the beginning).

Stage 2

The start - better desing

Stage 2 will be fixing bugs, thinking about more features, better desing for showcasing the stats and to clean some code and optimize some code. Some more back-end stuff for future implaments.

Stage 3

More stats!!

Yeahh you read it, once on stage 3 all "top" stats will go to top 50's :). This is also a stage where I prepare a lot of back-end code for stage 4 :).

Stage 4

The drop - extra features

The stage you have been waiting for. MORE FEATURES WOOP WOOP. You will be able to see all your playlists and edit them (remove and add tracks to it). Being able to search tracks/albums (yeah full albums), no more 1 by 1 adding songs to your playlists. Check your recent history of the tracks you played. Search a specific album, artist or track and checkt that specific statistic. Check your upcomming "hot" tracks and artist, this is a calculation by myself. Once you listin to a specific artist and/or track a lot in a short period it will get the state "hot", this will be a icon that will be displayed in your top lists.

Stage 5

The finish - but not the end

After stage 4, this will be the stage were I optimize and make some more advancements for the features. When this stage is released I see the site as "fully ready". This doesn't mean I will stop to support it. I will still think about more feature to add and ofcourse user feedback for more improvements/features.

Stage 6

The future

I'm not sure if this is possible, but in this stage I will check out if I can add a system to check other statistics like Youtube music/amazon music/Deezer.
